Finding the age

Joe is 2 times as old as his brother, Jack. Together, they have a combined age of 21 years. If Joe's age is x and Jack's age is represented by y, what is each child's age? Write and solve a system of linear equations the represent this situation by substitution.
First, set up two equations that represent this situation:
x=2y
x+y=21
Next, substitute the value of x from the first equation into the second equation:
2y+y=21
3y=21
y=7
Substitute the value of y back into one on the original equations:
x=2(7)
x=14
So, Joe is 14 years old and Jack is 7 years old.
